# 📊 R-Mart Sales Analysis Dashboard (POWER BI PROJECT)

## 📌 Project Overview

The **R-Mart Sales Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, product categories, payment methods, and regional performance. The dashboard provides business stakeholders with actionable insights to support data-driven decision-making.

This project demonstrates the use of Power BI for transforming raw sales data into meaningful visualizations and KPIs.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Monitor overall sales performance.
- Track profit trends across different months.
- Analyze top-performing states and customers.
- Identify profitable product sub-categories.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Evaluate payment mode preferences.
- Enable interactive filtering for detailed analysis.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard displays the following business metrics:

- **Total Sales Amount:** ₹438K
- **Total Profit:** ₹37K
- **Total Quantity Sold:** 6K
- **Average Order Value (AOV):** 120.9

---

### 🔹 Sales by State

Visualizes the **Top 5 States** contributing to total sales.

**Top Performing States:**
- Maharashtra
- Madhya Pradesh
- Uttar Pradesh
- Delhi
- Rajasthan

---

### 🔹 Monthly Profit Analysis

Tracks monthly profit performance throughout the year.

**Insights:**
- Highest profit observed in November.
- Losses recorded during May, July, September, and December.
- Helps identify seasonal business trends.

---

### 🔹 Category-wise Quantity Distribution

Analyzes product quantity sold across categories:

- Clothing
- Electronics
- Furniture

---

### 🔹 Payment Mode Analysis

Shows customer payment preferences.

**Payment Methods Included:**
- COD (Cash on Delivery)
- UPI
- Debit Card
- Credit Card

---

### 🔹 Top Customers Analysis

Identifies customers generating the highest revenue.

**Business Value:**
- Customer segmentation
- Loyalty program targeting
- Customer retention strategies

---

### 🔹 Profit by Sub-Category

Highlights the most profitable product sub-categories.

**Top Sub-Categories:**
- Printers
- Bookcases
- Saree
- Accessories
- Tables
